Volunteers are ready to sew masks to support you.

Submit a request. Fill out the form, below.

Instructions

Your facility is required to place a labeled container outside of your front door or at curbside to accept donated masks.

Once your request is filled, then you must:

  1. Send an email to pull your request.
  2. Remove the container from your facility
  3. Pass along any extra masks to another health center or laboratory in need

If you have any questions or need help, e-mail Nancy Gaus.
